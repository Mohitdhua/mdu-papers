80% se zyada score karna koi rocket science nahi hai. Bas thodi smart planning, consistency aur sahi strategy chahiye. Yahan hum 10 aise practical tips share kar rahe hain jo MDU students ke liye genuinely kaam karte hain.

## 1. Jaldi Shuru Karo, Last Minute Pe Mat Chhodo

Sabse bada difference consistency banati hai. Roz thoda-thoda padhne wala student raat-bhar jaagne wale student se hamesha aage rehta hai. Exam se 4-6 hafte pehle planning shuru kar do.

## 2. Syllabus Ko Units Mein Tod Do

Pura syllabus ek saath dekhoge toh overwhelming lagega. Ise chhote chunks (units/topics) mein divide karo aur har din ka ek realistic target set karo. Chhoti jeet motivation deti hai.

## 3. Previous Year Papers Solve Karo

Yeh repeat karne layak point hai — [PYQs](/courses) aapko batate hain ki exam mein kya aata hai. High-frequency topics pehle cover karo. Yeh single sabse high-ROI strategy hai.

## 4. Active Recall Use Karo

Sirf padhne se information yaad nahi rehti. Padhne ke baad book band karke khud se poocho: "Maine abhi kya padha?" Yeh "active recall" technique aapki memory ko 2x strong banati hai.

## 5. Notes Apne Words Mein Banao

Khud ke short notes banana sabse effective revision tool hai. Definitions, formulas aur key points ki ek concise sheet banao jise aap exam se pehle 10 minute mein revise kar sako.

## 6. Presentation Pe Dhyan Do

Examiner sirf content nahi, presentation bhi dekhta hai. Clean handwriting, headings, underline keywords, aur jahan possible ho wahan diagrams banao. Achhi presentation se 5-10% extra marks aaram se aate hain.

## 7. Time Management Practice Karo

Exam hall mein ghadi aapki dushman ban sakti hai. Timed mock tests do taaki aapko pata chale ki ek question pe kitna time dena hai. Har question ko marks ke proportion mein time do.

## 8. Compulsory Section Ko Master Karo

Short-answer / compulsory section easy marks ka source hai. Definitions, full forms aur basic concepts ko itna pakka karo ki yeh marks kabhi miss na ho.

## 9. Health Aur Neend Ignore Mat Karo

Raat bhar jaagne se exam mein dimaag kaam nahi karta. 7-8 ghante ki neend, halka khana aur thoda break — yeh sab aapke performance ko directly affect karte hain. Brain ko bhi rest chahiye.

## 10. Exam Se Pehle Calm Raho

Last raat naya topic mat shuru karo. Sirf revision karo, apni notes dekho, aur confident raho. Exam hall mein pehle aasaan questions solve karo taaki confidence build ho.

## Bonus: Group Study Smartly

Doston ke saath padhna helpful hai — par tabhi jab focus bana rahe. Ek doosre se concepts explain karwana (teaching) understanding ko kaafi deep kar deta hai.

## Conclusion

80%+ score karna discipline aur smart strategy ka combination hai. In 10 tips ko apni daily routine mein shaamil karo, PYQs se practice karo, aur apni health ka khayal rakho.
