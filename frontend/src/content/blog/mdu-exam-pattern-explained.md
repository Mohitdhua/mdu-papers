Bahut se students paper toh padh lete hain, par unhe exam ka **structure** hi clear nahi hota. Aur jab aapko nahi pata ki paper kaise aayega, toh preparation bhi direction-less ho jaati hai. Is article mein hum MDU semester exam pattern ko detail mein samjhenge taaki aap confidently exam de sako.

## MDU Exam Ka Basic Structure

MDU ke theory papers usually **maximum 75 ya 80 marks** ke hote hain (internal assessment alag se hoti hai). Exam ki duration generally **3 hours** hoti hai. Paper do major parts mein divided hota hai:

### Part A — Compulsory / Short Answer Questions

Yeh section compulsory hota hai aur isme short questions hote hain — definitions, one-liners, ya 2-3 line ke answers. Inka total weightage usually 15-20 marks hota hai. Yahan se easy marks milte hain, isliye inhe kabhi skip mat karo.

### Part B — Unit-Wise Long Answer Questions

Syllabus generally 4-5 units mein bata hota hai. Har unit se ek ya do questions aate hain, aur aapko **internal choice** milti hai (e.g. "Attempt any one"). Yeh long answer questions zyada marks ke hote hain.

## Marking Scheme Kaise Kaam Karta Hai

- **Internal Assessment:** Class tests, assignments aur attendance ke basis par (subject ke hisaab se 20-25 marks).
- **External (Theory) Exam:** Main semester exam (75-80 marks).
- **Passing Criteria:** Aapko theory aur internal dono milakar minimum passing marks laane hote hain (usually 40% aggregate).

Iska matlab — internal assessment ko halke mein mat lo. Yeh aapka safety cushion hai.

## Question Types Jo Aate Hain

1. **Definitions & Theory:** "Define normalization", "What is an operating system?"
2. **Long Explanatory:** "Explain different types of joins with examples."
3. **Numerical / Problem-Solving:** Maths, accounting aur technical subjects mein.
4. **Diagrams & Programs:** CS subjects mein code likhna ya diagram banana.
5. **Compare & Contrast:** "Difference between DBMS and RDBMS."

## Pattern Samajhne Ka Fayda

Jab aapko pattern pata hota hai, toh aap:

- **Time better manage** kar paate ho — kaunse question pe kitna time dena hai.
- **Compulsory section** ke liye ready rehte ho jahan easy marks hote hain.
- **Choice-based questions** ke liye selectively prepare kar sakte ho.
- Exam hall mein **panic nahi** karte kyunki sab kuch expected hota hai.

> Ek chhota sa secret: jin questions mein "explain with example" likha ho, wahan diagram ya example add karne se examiner ko impress karna aasaan hota hai.

## Pattern Kaise Confirm Karein

Sabse reliable tarika hai [previous year papers](/courses) dekhna. 3-4 papers dekhne se aapko exact structure, marks distribution aur question style samajh aa jayega. Official syllabus aur paper dono ko saath rakhkar study karo.
